## 4. Syntheses from o-alkynylanilines

The use of o-alkynylanilines is a part of a popular strategy for the synthesis of substituted indoles. o-Alkynylanilines are convenient substrates for annulation of a pyrrole ring to a benzene ring via intramolecular nucleophilic addition. 106 All methods of synthesis involving o-alkynylaniline derivatives can be split into several groups: (1) cyclization of ready o-alkynylaniline substrates under various conditions; (2) introduction of amine functions into the ortho-position of phenylacetylenes and their various uses; (3) utilization of the method to prepare functionalized indoles; (4) introduction of an ethynyl group into the ortho-position of the aniline molecule.
